BEGINNER READING TASK

Topic: My Favorite Breakfast
Instruction:
Read the text carefully and choose the best answer (A, B, C, or D) for each question.

Text

I love breakfast because it is the most important meal of the day. It gives me energy and helps me prepare for school and daily activities. Eating breakfast also helps me stay focused and active throughout the morning. That is why I never skip breakfast.


My favorite breakfast is cereal with milk. I like the crunchiness of the cereal and how it tastes with the cold milk. It is delicious, easy to prepare, and healthy. I usually eat cereal before going to school because it is quick and filling.


Sometimes, I also enjoy toast with jam. The sweetness of the jam on warm toast is comforting. My favorite flavor is strawberry jam because it tastes sweet and fresh. I often eat toast when I have more time in the morning.


Another breakfast food that I like is eggs. I usually eat scrambled eggs with a little salt. They are fluffy, tasty, and good for my health. I also like drinking a glass of orange juice because it is refreshing and helps me start my day well.


On special days, my mom makes pancakes for breakfast. They are fluffy and golden brown. I like to put syrup on them, and sometimes my mom adds fresh fruit such as bananas or strawberries. Pancakes are my favorite special breakfast because they are delicious and fun to eat.

1. Why does the writer love breakfast?

2. What is the writer's favorite breakfast?

3. How does the writer describe cereal?

4. What flavor of jam does the writer like most?

5. How does the writer usually eat eggs?

6. What drink does the writer enjoy?

7. Who makes pancakes on special days?

8. What does the writer put on pancakes?

9. What fruit may be added to the pancakes?

10. What is the main topic of the text?
